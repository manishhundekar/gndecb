About SWAYAM-NPTEL:

NPTEL (National Programme on Technology Enhanced Learning) is a joint initiative of IITs and IISc. It has been offering self-study courses across engineering, humanities and science streams for more than a decade. These are available at https://nptel.ac.in. From March 2014, NPTEL has been offering online certification for its courses, the highlight being the certification exam through which the student gets an opportunity to earn a certificate form the IITs. These are available at https://swayam.gov.in/NPTEL

NPTEL Local Chapter:

To take this initiative forward and to encourage more students of GNDEC Bidar to participate in this initiative, Mrs. Reshma Kaur, Vice Chairperson, GNDEC Bidar inspired for setting up NPTEL Local Chapter in the college under the NPTEL Nodal center at IIT Madras This local chapter is led by a faculty member of the college, who is single Point of Contact (SPOC) to NPTEL center at IIT Madras. GNDEC, being a ‘Local Chapter of NPTEL’ appeal you to take the benefits of this initiative and enjoy the E-Learning, where you can study as per your mood and play/pause/forward/backward/stop your teacher as per your grasping limit. The aim of this local chapter is to uplift the knowledge/ expertise level of students and faculty members to national/ international level.

The Central Library has a DTH antenna to receive the SWAYAM PRABHA group of 34 channels devoted to telecasting of high-quality educational programmes on 24×7 basis practicing the GSAT-15 satellite. The channels are uplinked from BISAG, Gandhinagar. The contents are provided by NPTEL, IITs, UGC, CEC, IGNOU, NCERT and NIOS. The INFLIBNET Centre maintains the web portal. Students and staff members can watch the MOOCs offered through SWAYAM.
