ABOUT EVENT
Guru Nanak Dev Engineering College ,Bidar affiliated by Visvesvaraya Technological University is honoured to organize 22nd VTU Inter-Collegiate Athletic Meet 2019-20, Wherein the students of all the affiliated engineering colleges of the university will be participating.

It is our privilege to organize this mega event in the beautiful campus of GNDEC which has developed all the state-of-the-art infrastructural facilities to organize the Athletic Events. We are happy to know that more than 200 Engineering colleges will be participating in this event. We hope that this event would be one of the biggest sporting spectacles to be held in the state of Karnataka.We are sure it would provide a platform to showcase the extraordinary talents of students in athletics. It is our special privilege to extend a very hearty welcome to all the participants and officials for the athletic meet.

ABOUT COLLEGE
Guru Nanak Dev Engineering College, Bidar, a premier center of Technical Education, was established in August, 1980 by the Prabandhak Committee, Gurudwara Sri Nanak Jhira Saheb, Bidar as per the Govt. of Karnataka Order No.ED 150 TEC 79, dated 8th August, 1980. At present College is being managed by Sri Nanak Jhira Saheb Foundation (Trust). Initially, the College was affiliated to Gulbarga University, Gulbarga. However, subsequently a separate Technical University Viz. Visvesvaraya Technological University, Belagavi, has been established during the academic year 1998-99, since then this College has been affiliated to the said University.

GNDEC started in the year 1980 with an annual intake of 120 in three traditional branches. The meticulous planning and foresight of the management has ensured that every facility is provided to the students. The excellent facilities and infrastructure are in the form of Administrative Building, Auditorium, Central Library, Separate Digital Library. The consistent high quality performance of its alumni has given the college an edge over other institutions thereby earning a reputation for honesty, integrity and innovative education.
