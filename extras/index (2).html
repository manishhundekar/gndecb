About GNDECB Education


Guru Nanak Dev Engineering College, Bidar, a premier center of Technical Education, was established in August, 1980.This College is approved by All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), New Delhi and Accredited by National Board of Accreditation (N.B.A.), New Delhi. Currently 2500 students all over from country benefited from a rare level of education imparted through professionals.
