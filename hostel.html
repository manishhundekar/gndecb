College Hostel

The college provides hostel accommodation (Comprising three wings) to 640 out-station (Boys) students in the college campus. The hostel also has a Gurudwara Saheb with regular prayers taking place.

A separate hostel for girl students is available in Gurudwara Sri Nanak Jhira Saheb complex, Bidar with all the amenities.

Indoor games, recreational facilities and a Gymnasium are provided in the hostels for students in both the hostels. To meet the academic requirements of the students in the hostel the college is providing round the clock internet facility.

A well balanced nutritional vegetarian food is provided in the mess and special care is taken in maintaining hygienic condition.

No un-authorized student/person is allowed to stay in the hostel. The students are to abide by the hostel rules and regulations supplied along with the hostel application form and as prescribed by the Management from time to time.
