  Message

Colonel Dr. B.S.Dhaliwal(Retd.)EME

Director Academics

One prominent parameter out of so many quality measures of academic process is providing the serene and secured atmosphere, conducive for the professional growth of the students. GNDEC provides an environment that fosters the intellectual, innovative and entrepreneurial growth of its students.

Eminence tutoring is the need of the day; GNDEC presents such an atmosphere that promotes quality education giving the students the values of academic uprightness, appreciation of intellectual excellence and creativity.

GNDEC is not only an icon of academic excellence, but also a centre for personality grooming and intellectual development. GNDEC is the fountainhead of learning and advancement. The institution also helps to bring out the hidden qualities of students through its integrated cocurricular and extra curricular activities.

I hope and wish GNDEC will do its utmost in preparing dynamic engineers and responsible citizens motivated to serve the country and humanity.

It is a matter of enormous satisfaction that GNDEC is fulfilling its responsibilities by providing the quality technical education for the brighter future of the country.

I wish all the students a very best during their stay at GNDEC and in all their endeavors after passing out.
